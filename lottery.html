How The Lottery Works

Low-Roller Lottery:

  • Prize: 0.5 7STAR
  • Entries: 7 participants
  • Entry fee: 0.08333 7STAR per entry
  • Total collected: 0.58333 7STAR (0.5 for prize, 0.08333 as fee)

High-Roller Lottery:

  • Prize: 1 7STAR
  • Entries: 7 participants
  • Entry fee: 0.16667 7STAR per entry
  • Total collected: 1.16667 7STAR (1 for prize, 0.16667 as fee)

Prize Distribution:

  • When a lottery is filled with 7 entries, a winner is randomly selected
  • Prize is sent directly to the winner's wallet
  • Entry fees are held in a program-controlled wallet until distribution
  • Winners are notified via Telegram

Random Selection:

Winners are selected randomly when all 7 entries are filled. Selection is transparent and verifiable on-chain.

Fees

Entry fees are used to fund the prize pool and cover transaction costs. A small portion will be allocated for StarCycle's Strategic 7STAR Reserve.
