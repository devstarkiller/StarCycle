What is StarCycle?

StarCycle is a fractional staking protocol that turns stardust—small pieces of 7STAR—into a spinning engine of RAPR rewards. Here’s how it works:

The Pool

StarCycle is the ultimate test of conviction—a high-stakes, all-in flywheel for believers in 7STAR’s scarcity. Here’s how it spins:

Step 1: Deposit Your Stardust

Users contribute stardust in increments of 0.1 (e.g., 0.1, 0.2, 0.3 7STAR) to the StarCycle pool. This stardust is locked—removing it from circulation—shrinking 7STAR’s scarce supply of 752 tokens. Once the pool gathers enough to stake 1 full 7STAR, the flywheel starts.

Step 2: Full Compounding Commitment

All earned RAPR is staked and compounded every Happy Hour, until enough RAPR is earned to buy an additional 7STAR token. No payouts—no exceptions. Compounding continues until the pool accrues 10 7STARs.

Step 3: The Faith Vote

Once 10 7STARs are accrued, contributors vote:

- Withdraw: Stop the cycle—send full stars to contributors, proportional to their initial deposit.

- Keep Spinning: Push to 20 7STARs, cast another vote once achieved.
